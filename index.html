IEEE Women in Engineering International Leadership Summits (ILS) provide regional opportunities to foster networking, mentorship, and collaboration. With the leadership and creativity of IEEE WIE volunteers, WIE Summits have been extremely successful and have made an outstanding impact on communities around the globe!

IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) is one of the largest international professional organizations dedicated to promoting women engineers and scientists, and inspiring girls around the world to follow their academic interests in a career in engineering. The mission of IEEE WIE is to facilitate the recruitment and retention of women in technical disciplines globally.

About the Event

Al Akhawayn University in Ifrane, Morocco, is hosting this year's IEEE Women In Engineering International Leadership Summit on November 16th and 17th, 2023. The primary objective of this summit is to bring together women who are actively involved in the fields of engineering, technology, and science from around the world. It provides them with a platform to not only share their knowledge, experiences, and innovative ideas but also to empower them to take on leadership roles within their respective domains.

The summit's theme, "Empowering Women in Technology: Cultivating Innovation for Sustainable Impact and Entrepreneurship," emphasizes the pivotal and influential role that women occupy in shaping the future landscape of engineering and technology. The event will showcase prominent keynote speakers. Additionally, there will be panel discussions, a Hackathon, and opportunities for networking.

This summit presents valuable chances to connect with peers, both women and men, who are engaged in STEM fields. It fosters the exchange of insights and the acquisition of knowledge from experts within a supportive and collaborative environment.

You are kindly invited to register and attend this year’s edition!

Venue

Al Akhawayn University (AUI) in Ifrane is a prestigious institution located in the serene city of Ifrane, Morocco. Known for its commitment to academic excellence and international education standards, AUI offers a diverse range of programs that foster intellectual growth and intercultural understanding. With a beautiful campus nestled in the Atlas Mountains, AUI provides a unique and conducive learning environment. The university's emphasis on critical thinking, research, and community engagement prepares students to become well-rounded global citizens who contribute positively to society.
