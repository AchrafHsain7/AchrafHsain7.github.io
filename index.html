Hello, World

I'm a PhD student passionate about pushing the boundaries of machine learning and artificial intelligence. My research focuses on developing novel approaches to reinforcement learning and understanding how attention mechanisms can improve learning efficiency in complex environments.

When I'm not training models or debugging code, you'll find me exploring new ideas at the intersection of theory and practice, always seeking elegant solutions to challenging problems. I believe in open science and sharing knowledge with the community.
