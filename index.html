Hello, World

I'm a PhD student exploring the mathematical foundations of intelligent decision-making. My research spans reinforcement learning, information theory, and optimization — seeking principled approaches to building agents that learn effectively from experience.

From Bellman equations to policy gradients, I'm fascinated by how mathematical elegance translates into practical algorithms. When I'm not deriving bounds or debugging reward signals, you'll find me exploring the intersection of theory and practice.
